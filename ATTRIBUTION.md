## Pali Dictionary

### Nyanatiloka's Buddhist Dictionary

**Author:** Nyanatiloka Mahathera (1878–1957)  
**Status:** Public domain (author died more than 70 years ago)  
**Available at:** https://www.accesstoinsight.org/lib/authors/nyanatiloka/wheel238.html

**What we use:**
- Primary source for Pali term definitions
- Explanations of Buddhist concepts
- Cross-references between terms

**About the author:** Nyanatiloka Mahathera was a German-born Theravada Buddhist monk who became one of the foremost Western scholars of Pali and Buddhist philosophy. His Buddhist Dictionary remains a standard reference.

---

### Pali Text Society Dictionary

**Authors:** T.W. Rhys Davids and William Stede  
**Published:** 1921–1925  
**Status:** Public domain (published before 1925)

**What we use:**
- Etymological information
- Word roots and derivations
- Cross-references

**Note:** We use only the original public domain text, not modern copyrighted editions or additions.

## Goenka Tradition Content

### Our Approach

The notes labelled "In the Goenka Tradition" throughout Dhamma Mirror represent the author's personal understanding of the emphasis placed on certain concepts within the S.N. Goenka / Sayagyi U Ba Khin tradition of Vipassana meditation.

**These notes are:**
- Written in the author's own words
- Based on personal experience attending courses
- Not official VRI content
- Not endorsed by dhamma.org or VRI

**For authoritative teachings in this tradition, please refer to:**

| Resource | URL |
|----------|-----|
| **Dhamma.org** | https://www.dhamma.org |
| **Vipassana Research Institute** | https://www.vridhamma.org |
| **Pariyatti** | https://store.pariyatti.org |
| **Dhamma.org App** | iOS App Store / Google Play (for old students) |

---

## What We Do NOT Use

To be clear about our boundaries:

| Content | Status |
|---------|--------|
| **Goenkaji's discourses** | NOT reproduced — available only through official channels |
| **VRI copyrighted publications** | NOT reproduced — available at Pariyatti |
| **Course materials** | NOT reproduced — attend a course at dhamma.org |
| **Chanting recordings** | NOT reproduced — available in official app |

We respect the intellectual property of VRI and direct users to official sources for authoritative content.
